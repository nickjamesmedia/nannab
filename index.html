Nanna B’s Sourdough

Fresh, Homemade Sourdough from Nanna’s Kitchen

I’m Crystal (but to my grandkids, just Nanna). I bake sourdough breads and other creations right here in Fort Macleod, Alberta. I bake when I’m home from camping and not chasing after my grandkids, so orders open based on availability. All baking is done in my home kitchen. Weekly availability is posted on Facebook.

Products

  • Artisanal round or sandwich loaves — plain or whole wheat
  • Loaded loaves & focaccia (8x8" & 9x13"): Cinnamon Raisin, Cranberry Walnut, Cheddar Rosemary, Cinnamon Swirl, Jalapeño Cheddar, Parmesan & Garlic, Italian Herb & Cheddar
  • Monthly flavours (see Facebook)
  • 4‑pack sample (1 plain + 3 assorted)
  • Double chocolate loaf
  • Chocolate chip sourdough cookies (one dozen, real butter)

Dairy‑free loaves available on request. Two special flavours are offered each month and announced on Facebook.
